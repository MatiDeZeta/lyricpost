Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

LyricPost is a free lyrics image generator. We do not require accounts and do not sell personal data.

Data stored on your device

We use your browser’s localStorage to save preferences you choose to keep:

  • Image customization settings
  • Recent searches (up to 6)
  • Export history with compressed thumbnails (up to 30 entries)
  • Custom templates

This data stays on your device unless you clear site data. Custom cover uploads are session-only and are not persisted.

Third-party services

When you search or paste a music link, LyricPost may contact:

  • Last.fm — song metadata (via our server proxy)
  • lrclib — lyrics
  • Spotify, YouTube, Apple/iTunes, Deezer — link metadata and artwork where applicable

Those services receive the queries or URLs needed to fulfill your request. Please review their policies separately.

Analytics

We may use privacy-friendly analytics (e.g. Plausible) to count page views and feature usage. No advertising trackers or cross-site profiling are used.
